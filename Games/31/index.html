31 / Blitz

Create a table or join with a room code. Your device remembers your seat if you refresh.

How to Play 31

Each player starts with 3 cards. On your turn, draw from the new pile or dead pile, then discard back down to 3 cards.

Your score is the best total you can make in one suit. Aces are 11, face cards are 10, and number cards keep their value.

Tap a card to select it. Swipe the selected card upward toward the table or press Play a Card after drawing.
