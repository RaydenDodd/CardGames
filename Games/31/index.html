Create a table or join with a room code. Rejoin with the same name to reclaim your hand.

How to Play 31

Each player starts with 3 cards. Your goal is to make the highest total in one suit, up to 31.

On your turn, draw one card from the New pile or the Dead Pile, then discard one card so your hand returns to 3 cards.

Scoring only counts one suit. Aces are 11, face cards are 10, and number cards keep their value. Your Best shows your strongest suit total.

Before drawing, you may Check if you have 3 cards. Checking ends your turn and gives every other player one final turn.

The game ends after all final turns are complete, or immediately when the New pile runs out. The final scoreboard reveals every hand, score, and difference from first place.

The host can reset the round, play again after scores, or end the room for everyone.
